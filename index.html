How Nothing Days Work
Each year earns 1 or 2 Nothing Days based on whether a Gregorian leap day falls within that year's 364-day window. These days are banked silently — never taken individually. When the bank reaches a multiple of 7, a Nothing Timeline is released at the end of that year. Because it is always a multiple of 7, it occupies complete weeks and the very next day is always Monday — Day 1 of the new year, with zero drift.
